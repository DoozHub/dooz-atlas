# Glossary

Common terms used in DoozieSoft AI documentation.

---

## A

**Agentic AI**
AI systems capable of autonomous multi-step actions with tool use.

**AG-Guard**
DoozieSoft's guardrail plugin for agent safety.

## C

**CODEOWNER**
Person responsible for maintaining a documentation section.

**Context Window**
Maximum tokens an LLM can process in one request.

## G

**Guardrail**
Safety mechanism that limits what agents can do.

## H

**HITL (Human in the Loop)**
Pattern requiring human approval for certain actions.

**Hallucination**
AI generating plausible but incorrect information.

## L

**LLM (Large Language Model)**
Foundation models used for text generation and understanding.

## M

**Mechanical Task**
Routine, pattern-based work suitable for cheap AI.

## P

**Prompt Library**
Collection of approved, versioned prompts.

## R

**RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)**
Combining search with LLM generation.

## S

**SOP (Standard Operating Procedure)**
Mandatory rules that must be followed.

## T

**Tier**
Classification of AI models by capability and cost.
- Tier 1: Fast/cheap (mechanical tasks)
- Tier 2: Standard (cognitive tasks)
- Tier 3: Thinking (architectural tasks)
- Tier 4: Adversarial (security review)

**Token**
Basic unit of text for LLM processing (~4 characters).
